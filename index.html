About Ruaka

Est. January 2026

Where It
All Began

Ruaka Wig Spa & Wellness Hub was founded in January 2026 with a single, powerful mission: give every wig a second life and every woman a place to feel truly pampered.

Located in the heart of Ruaka — kwa Bumps — we launched as a wig laundry and revamp service and quickly grew into a full-service beauty and wellness destination. Within months of opening, we had serviced over 400 wigs and built a loyal community of over 300 clients across Nairobi and Kiambu.

Our team of 5+ qualified professionals brings expertise, warmth and precision to every appointment — whether you're dropping off a wig, coming in for a massage, or getting a fresh set of braids.

We believe beauty is not a luxury — it's confidence. And confidence starts at the crown.
